# of Cloudflare's newer `Content-Signal:` extension because Lighthouse and
# most third-party SEO validators don't yet recognise Content-Signal as a
# valid directive — they flag it as "Unknown directive" and downgrade the
# SEO audit score, even though the directive is semantically valid.

# This list mirrors what Cloudflare's AI Audit feature auto-injected, but
# uses the Disallow form that every robots.txt validator understands.
