Common Questions

Why didn't my page update?

Ensure the Jira ticket is in the "Done" column and that it is explicitly linked to the Confluence page using the "Link issue" field.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use AWS and Google Cloud Enterprise APIs in Europe. Your data is never used for model training.
