Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 20, 2025

Data Sovereignty (GDPR)

EcoSynch is built with European Data Sovereignty as a core requirement.

  • Storage: All metadata is stored in AWS eu-west-1 (Ireland).
  • AI Processing: All text processing occurs in Google Cloud europe-west1 (Belgium).
  • No Transfers: Your data never leaves the European Economic Area (EEA) during processing.

Data We Process

We only process data when explicitly triggered by a Jira event:

  • Jira Tickets: Summary, Description, and Comments of tickets moved to "Done."
  • Confluence Pages: The content of pages explicitly linked to those tickets.

We do not scan your entire instance or access data unrelated to the specific ticket being processed.

AI Training

Your data is NOT used to train AI models. We use the Enterprise APIs of Google Vertex AI, which contractually guarantees that inputs and outputs are not used for model training.

Subprocessors

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Hosting & Database (Ireland).
  • Google Cloud Platform: AI Inference (Belgium).
