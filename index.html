The "Definition of Done" is Broken.

Engineers love shipping code. They hate updating the Wiki.

The moment a feature is released, your documentation becomes a legacy artifact. It describes how the system used to work, not how it works now.

This "Documentation Rot" creates a trust gap. Support teams give wrong answers, new hires get confused, and Product Managers spend hours manually auditing pages.

Built for Engineering Velocity

We replaced the "Manual Update" ticket with an intelligent agent.

Surgical Rewriting

Traditional integrations just slap a "Jira Macro" at the top of the page. That's noise, not information.

EcoSynch uses semantic vector search (RAG) to find the exact paragraph that contradicts your new code. It surgically rewrites just those 3 sentences to match the new reality, preserving your original tone, formatting, and structure.

Lazy Indexing™

We don't scrape your entire wiki. That's invasive and expensive. EcoSynch only indexes a page on-demand when you link a ticket to it. This keeps our permissions low and your security team happy.

EU Hosted Infrastructure

All AI processing occurs strictly within Google Cloud's europe-west1 region (Belgium). Your proprietary data never crosses the Atlantic.

Total Transparency

AI shouldn't be a black box. Every time EcoSynch updates a page, it creates a new Confluence Version and leaves a detailed comment:
"EcoSynch Update: Updated Python version references based on ticket SCRUM-123."

Your team can revert instantly if the AI gets it wrong (though it rarely does).
